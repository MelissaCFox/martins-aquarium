Proper Tank Setup

Regular Tank Maintenance

  1. Remove uneaten food
  2. Rinse the collection cup
  3. Wipe away any salt creep you see

Bi-Weekly Cleaning

  1. Remove 25% of water
  2. Clean te panes of glass
  3. Vacuum the substrate
  4. Wipe the protein skimmer
  5. Rinse the prefilters
  6. Add new salt water to the tank

Long Term Cleaning

  1. Clean the aquarium and lighting fixture covers
  2. Clean the air intake hose and skimmer
  3. Deep-clean the aquarium every few months, focusing on: protein skimmer, return pump, biofilter overflow box, powerhead, heater, thermometer, and hoses

Proper Salinity

Saltwater aquariums should maintain a salinity between 1.020-1.028sg. Fish only aquariums may be kept at lower levels (1.017+) to keep salt costs down and reduce potential parasites.

Proper Water Temperature

Water temperature should be maintained between 78-80F for most fish to thrive. Many coral may prefer slightly cooler temps (78F).
